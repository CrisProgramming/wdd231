Site Plan: Transformational Healing

Site Name

Transformational Healing
This name reflects the mission to help people heal from emotional wounds and align mind, body, emotions, and spirit through coaching sessions, workshops, and spiritual tools.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote transformational coaching services, provide resources for emotional healing, describe upcoming events and workshops, and allow visitors to book sessions or contact the coach.

Scenarios

  • How can I heal emotional wounds that are affecting my relationships?
  • Where can I sign up for the next emotional healing workshop?

Color Schema

  • Soft Pink (#ffe4ec): Background and header/footer accents.
  • Deep Rose (#ff6f91): Headings and call-to-action buttons.

Typography

  • 'Poppins', sans-serif: Used for all headings.
  • 'Open Sans', sans-serif: Used for body text and paragraphs.
